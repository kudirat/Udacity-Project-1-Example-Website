Hello! Welcome to my Shows Recommendations blog! Here I'll be covering a few shows that I've been watching that I'd recommend others to try too!

SpyxFamily

A Slice of Life Comedy about a spy, telepath, and assasin, and how they form a fake family for the sake of world peace! This characters are highly entertaining and the art is very eye-catching!

Kotaro Lives Alone

A Slice of Life Comedy that sometimes reminds you that it's a little tragic (that's sugar-coating at this point). The main character, Kotaro, despite his back story being really heart-breaking, is a great role-model when it comes to perserverance.

Jojo's Bizarre Adventures

If you're looking for a plot that'll have you walking in circles in confusion and entertainment, I'd definitely recommend Jojo's Bizarre Adventures. Despite the beginning which is slow, but without a doubt necessary, this show will have you practicing the most absurd back-bending poses in your bathroom mirror.
